Diabetic Prediction

Diabetes is a growing global health concern and one of the leading chronic diseases affecting people of all ages. Unlike other conditions, diabetes often progresses silently, making early detection challenging. Machine learning techniques have been developed to analyze symptoms to assist in early prediction. These AI-assisted methods improve screening efficiency and help raise awareness before severe complications occur.
