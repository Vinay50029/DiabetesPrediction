Diabetic Retinopathy Stages

  • No DR: No signs of diabetic retinopathy.
  • Mild DR: Microaneurysms present; early sign of DR.
  • Moderate DR: Blood vessels in the retina are damaged and may leak.
  • Severe DR: More blood vessels are blocked, reducing oxygen supply.
  • Proliferative DR: New abnormal blood vessels grow; high risk of vision loss.
