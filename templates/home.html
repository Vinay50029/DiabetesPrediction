Welcome to the Diabetes Prediction

This system helps in the early detection of diabetes through two methods: symptom-based analysis and retina image-based detection of diabetic retinopathy. Just enter your symptoms or upload retina images and get instant predictions powered by Machine Learning and Deep Learning models. Start by selecting a prediction method from the menu.
